ABOUT US



"Cassablanca" was born from a mission for eating like at Mom's House.



Using natural flavours and the best Bio-Ingredients, we proudly created "MODERN ROMANIAN" our Signature Cuisine.


We offer aperitives, main dishes, desserts and beverages in a sophisticated, fresh-casual setting. Our versatile menu is rooted in traditional Romanian recipes and naturally accommodates a variety of preferences including vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free.
